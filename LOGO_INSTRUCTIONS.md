## Instructions to Add Your Logo

1. **Save the Milan Enterprises logo image** that you provided to the `public` folder with the filename `milan-logo.png`

2. **File Path**: `public/milan-logo.png`

3. **Recommended Image Specifications**:

   - Size: 200x200 pixels or similar square ratio
   - Quality: High resolution for crisp display
